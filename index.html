AI Ping Pong Game







Instructions

  1. First Keep Your Laptop Screen Straight
  2. Move Approximately 3-4 Feet Away From Your Laptop
  3. Move Your Right Wrist In An Ascending Or Descending Order, a Red Dot Should Appear On Your Wrist
  4. Now Press The Play Button And Again Move 3-4 Feet Away From Your Laptop
  5. The Red Paddle Is Your Paddle, It Will Move According To Your Right Wrist
  6. Enjoy!
